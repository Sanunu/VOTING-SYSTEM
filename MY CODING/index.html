2 Easy Online Voting In Just 3 Steps

The ODC Voting System is extremely easy to use and navigate to, simply create an account here and get started with your first online voting, After a quick Registration, all you need to do is choose the prefered type of online voting (find out more about our offerings here). Then, depending on the option you have choosen, you can create digitalballot papers, your electoral register, voter groups, list of candidates for nomination, or customize your voter invitations, Moreover, you can track the voter turnout of your online voting in real time, customize the design and interface, and even develop additional individual features
